What to Do on Taniti

Volcano Adventure

Explore our active volcano with expert guides. Safe viewing points and educational tours about volcanic geology and local legends.

Learn More

Beach Paradise

White sandy beaches around Yellow Leaf Bay. Perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and watching spectacular sunsets.

Rainforest Exploration

Zip-line through tropical canopy, hiking trails, and guided eco-tours. Discover exotic wildlife and lush vegetation.

Fishing Tours

Chartered fishing trips in our safe harbor. Experience traditional Tanitian fishing methods and enjoy fresh catch.

Essential Information

  • Currency: US Dollar (EUR/YEN accepted)
  • Language: English widely spoken
  • Power: 120V (US standard outlets)
  • Safety: Very low crime, hospital available
  • Drinking age: 18 years
  • Many national holidays - check calendar

Accommodation Options

  • 4-star luxury resort with full amenities
  • Family-owned hotels (most popular choice)
  • Charming bed & breakfasts
  • Budget-friendly hostel for backpackers
  • All accommodations regularly inspected
  • Book early for best availability

Dining Options

  • 5 local restaurants (fresh fish & rice)
  • 3 American-style dining establishments
  • 2 Pan-Asian cuisine restaurants
  • 2 supermarkets & smaller grocery stores
  • 24-hour convenience store
  • Local microbrewery in Merriton Landing

Transportation

  • Public buses: 5am-11pm daily service
  • Rental cars available near airport
  • Taxis available in Taniti City
  • Bike rentals (helmets required by law)
  • Taniti City & Merriton Landing walkable
  • Private buses serve rural island areas
